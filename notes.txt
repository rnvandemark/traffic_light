ARDUINO: HOW TO COMPILE
- sketch -> upload compiled binary (CTRL + ALT + S)

ARDUINO: HOW TO UPLOAD
- https://support.arduino.cc/hc/en-us/articles/4404168794514-Reset-the-firmware-on-Nano-RP2040-Connect
- jumper wire between GND and REC (2nd and 3rd to bottom left pins)
- preset reset button
- remove jumper wire
- copy .ino.elf.uf2 file to mass storage folder
